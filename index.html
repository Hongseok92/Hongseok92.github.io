I am master student of economics and finance at Hanyang University. My research lies between macroeconomics, labor economics and applied econometrics. I am professor of economics and economic history at the department of economics at Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München (Munich). At the department, I am co-PI in all programs that fund our graduate school, the MGSE (see here and here), and in our collaborative research center SFB/CRC TRR 190. I currently organize our department seminar, the Hans Möller Seminar, and serve as placement director.

I am the PI for ERC Starting Grant 716837 ("Demand for Democracy"), member of the Editorial Board of the Review of Economic Studies, associate editor at the Journal of the European Economic Association, and member of the editorial board of Explorations in Economic History.
